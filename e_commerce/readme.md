# E-commerce Sales Analysis

**Goal:** Analyze an Amazon-style e-commerce dataset to identify top-selling products, revenue trends, seasonality, and customer behavior for actionable business insights.

**Tools:** Python (pandas, matplotlib, seaborn), SQL, Tableau

**Dataset:** Synthetic transactional dataset simulating real-world online retail operations. Includes customer information, product details, transaction records, pricing, and order timelines. *(Sample CSV included; full dataset available upon request)*

---

## Key Findings

### Top Performing Categories
- **Bags & Storage** and **Home Decor / Garden Utility** generate the highest revenue.
- **Insight:** Core revenue drivers with consistent demand.
- **Recommendation:** Expand inventory and develop bundled product offerings to increase basket size and revenue.

### Seasonality
- Revenue and customer count peak during autumn and early winter (Oct–Nov).
- **Insight:** Clear seasonality aligned with holidays and celebrations.
- **Recommendation:** Boost marketing spend before Q4, launch seasonal collections, and prepare inventory in advance.

### High Average Order Value (£533)
- Customers purchase multiple items per transaction, despite low unit prices.
- **Insight:** Strong cross-product purchasing behavior.
- **Recommendation:** Introduce themed bundles, volume discounts, and cross-selling strategies at checkout.

### Event-Driven Popular Products
- Popular items are decorative / mood-enhancing (home decor, themed goods).
- **Insight:** Revenue peaks are tied to holidays, birthdays, weddings, anniversaries.
- **Recommendation:** Target marketing around life events and promote gift-focused bundles.

---

**Summary:**  
The business shows strong performance in home/decor categories, significant Q4 seasonality, and high AOV driven by multi-item purchases. Strategic bundling, event-based marketing campaigns, and inventory planning can further increase revenue and customer retention.
