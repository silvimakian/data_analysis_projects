# Customer Segmentation (RFM + Clustering)

**Goal:** Segment customers based on purchasing behavior to identify high-value and at-risk groups.

**Tools:** Python (pandas, scikit-learn), SQL, Tableau

**Dataset:** Customer transaction data including purchase history, order frequency, and monetary value. *(Sample CSV included; full dataset available upon request)*

---

## Key Findings

### Customer Segments
- Applied RFM (Recency, Frequency, Monetary) metrics and clustering to categorize customers.
- **Segments Identified:** High-Value, At-Risk, Loyal, and Occasional Buyers.
- **Recommendation:** Target high-value customers with retention campaigns; engage at-risk customers to reduce churn.

### Purchasing Trends
- High-value customers account for a significant portion of revenue.
- **Insight:** Retaining these customers is critical for business sustainability.
- **Recommendation:** Develop personalized promotions and loyalty rewards to strengthen retention.

### Engagement Insights
- Occasional buyers have potential for conversion.
- **Insight:** Opportunities exist to nurture these users into higher-value segments.
- **Recommendation:** Use targeted marketing and cross-selling strategies.

---

**Summary:**  
The segmentation revealed actionable customer groups. High-value and at-risk customers can be addressed with targeted strategies to improve retention, revenue, and long-term growth.
